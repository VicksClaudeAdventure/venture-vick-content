## Writing Process

1. **Check the latest research** in `content/research/` — use a proven hook from the most recent breakout analysis
2. **Match the blueprint format** to the Recovery episode type (usually storytelling format)
3. **Write the script** matching Day 1's energy and voice
4. **Include the counter** — show the old number, then the new number. The counter drop is the visual punchline.
5. **End card:** Bold counter overlay showing the updated amount remaining
6. **Run the 10x rewrite** from the scriptwriting skill's QC section
7. **Save to** `content/scripts/[YYYY-WW]/recovery/`
8. **Update** `content/RECOVERY_LOG.md` with the new episode details

## Rules

- **Counter only moves when money does.** No filler episodes.
- **Every episode uses a proven hook** from the latest research.
- **Honest about scale.** $250 isn't life-changing. Say that. The honesty is the brand.
- **Connect to the viewer.** "Odds are you've done this too." Marcus has wasted money on the same stuff.
- **No motivational energy.** Determined, not inspirational. The counter speaks for itself.
