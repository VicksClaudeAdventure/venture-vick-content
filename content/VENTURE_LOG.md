## Week 0 — The Decision (Before March 9)

### Beat: Five ideas, one AI decision
- Had 5 business ideas, couldn't pick one
- Dumped all 5 into an AI chat and said "don't hype me up — tell me what would actually work"
- AI scored them on: would people pay, how crowded is it, can I build it alone
- 4 got eliminated in 12 minutes
- The winner wasn't Jay's favorite — he almost left it off the list
- Winner: RentShield (paste your lease, get risky lines flagged in plain English)
- **Status:** 📝 (old Day 1 Venture script exists)
- **Emotional core:** Humbling — the AI picked the one I was least excited about, and it was right

### Beat: The odds conversation
- Asked AI for real odds of hitting $10K/month in 12 months
- Answer: 35-40% chance
- Sat with that number for a full hour
- Realized: one in three isn't zero, most people never take the swing
- Wrote a must-do checklist and sent it to the AI to build
- Left for a 5-day trip
- **Status:** 📝 (old Day 2 Venture script exists)
- **Emotional core:** Fear and defiance — the odds were bad and I went anyway
