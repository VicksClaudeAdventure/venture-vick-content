If you ramble or freeze when you speak, try this one easy technique.
Step one, use a random word generator to find a topic.
Step two, record yourself talking about it for two minutes.
In whatever you do, do not stop or restart.
Step three, watch it back and note your best idea.
Step four, edit it down to 30 to 60 seconds.
This 10-minute practice trains your mind to mouth
connection so that you can sound more articulate
and you also have a video to post.
