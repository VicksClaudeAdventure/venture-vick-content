Growing up divorced parents made me scared of one thing. Not cheating, not fighting, but losing the spark.
Being a long-term relationship though taught me something important.
The spark doesn't magically disappear overnight.
Instead it fades through consistent decisions of neglect, missed effort, choosing comfort over those tough conversations.
Love is just like everything else in your life.
It's built by actively choosing what you want.
That's why I still ask for it to be my Valentine every single year.
And why I still make this surprise.
It's one of the many things she absolutely deserves.
And honestly, I wouldn't be the person I am today without.
I'm just lucky she's still Jesus to say yes.
