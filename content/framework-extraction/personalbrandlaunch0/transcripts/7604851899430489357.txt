What's the difference between natural and unnatural
engaging formats?
Well, unnatural engaging formats.
Are the ones you can tell the video was obviously filmed?
Slap scripted to be engaging for the viewer.
Think visual formats, talking back at fourth format,
shot slash angle change format, or clone format.
Natural formats are so undercover,
you can't even tell a format is actually
being used to keep you engaged.
Think multitasking format, voice over format,
one person or multiple person Q&A format, and so on.
Different formats work well for different types of videos.
