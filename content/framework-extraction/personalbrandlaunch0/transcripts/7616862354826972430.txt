Did you know there are five different types of stories?
Number one, personal stories.
Sharing things about your life, feelings, or daily activities
so people can get to know you better.
Number two, sales paid offer stories.
Showing case studies results, testimonials,
perform afters, et cetera,
and then plugging a link to your paid offer.
Number three, sales stories for a lead magnet.
Promoting something valuable that's free
to get people's email or contact information.
Number four, authority stories.
Posting stories showing you know what you're doing
by sharing results, experiences, success, or knowledge.
Number five, educational stories.
Teaching people something useful
that helps them learn something or do better.
You'll probably post a mix of these,
but I'm gonna recommend predominantly sales stories
and maybe personal one here and there.
