Did you know that there are eight types of carousel?
Number one, the tutorial carousel.
Showing a niche step-by-step process,
system acronym, or framework.
Number two, the dues versus downed
or right versus wrong, old versus new carousel.
Where you show what to do versus not to do
to achieve a niche dream result.
Number three, an educational tips slash hack carousel.
This is just a one-off educational niche tips slash hack.
Number four, a storytelling carousel.
Where you tell a story in each slide
shows a new line and image from that time.
Number five, a transformation carousel.
Where you show before and after result
and explain the step-by-step how you got there.
Number six, a problem solution carousel.
This is where you share a niche problem
and then a solution to that problem
over and over again on a carousel.
Number seven, list carousels.
Each slide shows a new point on your list
and number eight, eight types carousel.
This is when you show multiple different types
of one niche related thing.
Now believe it or not,
there are way more than eight types of carousels.
These are just some popular ones I have been seeing work.
