Here's the perfect highlights formula.
Step number one, create the five main highlights.
To create a highlight, you wanna go to your profile,
click plus news, select any stories you wanna add
to your highlight, click next, name highlight,
and click add.
Using that process, you can create these five highlights.
Step number two, add stories to each highlight.
For the Welcome Slash start, here highlight,
I recommend posting a little introduction video
and fun facts about you picture.
For the reviews, slash results highlight,
I recommend posting client or personal
before and after image results.
For the packages, slash offers highlight,
I recommend posting a story breaking down
your main offer slash products, slash service.
For your tips slash hack highlight, post random one off
niche tips slash hacks.
For the FAQ highlight, this one is optional.
Find and answer common questions
you get in your comment section.
Step number three, add highlight covers.
To create them, go into Canva and search highlight covers,
choose a template, edit them to be your own,
and download them.
To edit the highlight covers on Instagram,
click the highlight, click the three dots,
click edit highlight, and click edit cover.
