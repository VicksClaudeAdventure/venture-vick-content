There are three types of content you should post.
Tofu, Mofu, and Bofu.
If you want more views and followers,
you need to create broad tofu content,
top of funnel, that makes a lot of people interested.
Recommended content types for this would be
ranking videos, rating videos,
tier list videos, Q&A type videos,
my story videos, and not super in-depth educational content.
Now, if you have a solid follower base
and strong reach, if you wanna build a strong connection
with your audience and create authority,
you need to create Mofu content, middle of funnel.
Recommended content types for this would be
in-depth educational step-by-step tutorials,
and storytelling videos about personal and business wins,
losses, and experiences.
If you already have a strong connection
and authority with your audience
and want to generate leads,
that would mean you need to create Bofu content.
Bottom of funnel.
Recommended content types for this would be
personal before and after transformations,
client before and after transformations,
fake case studies of what you would do for a celebrity
or a big brand, or a fake what I would do
in xyz situation videos.
Depending on your goals will determine
what type of content you're actually going to create,
but I would say for the most part,
most creators are gonna create a mix of all three.
Follow more social media marketing, education.
