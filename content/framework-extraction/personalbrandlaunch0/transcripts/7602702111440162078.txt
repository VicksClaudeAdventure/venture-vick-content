Trending audio plus b-roll plus text videos should be around three to seven seconds.
Q&A, ranking, or rating videos should be around 15 to 30 seconds.
Light educational videos with a repeatable or simple structure like this should be 15 to 30 seconds.
Storytelling videos should be around 45 to 60 seconds.
Hypereducational videos, slash tutorials should be around 45 to 90 seconds.
Note, none of these are hard and fast rules because there's always going to be exceptions.
