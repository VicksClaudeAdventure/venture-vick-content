How to create a viral carousel in 60 seconds.
Step number one, find your viral post.
Go to your Instagram profile, click the Rails tab twice,
click Most Viewed, and find the most viral post
on your account.
Choose one of these for your next carousel.
Step number two, choose a carousel type.
Pick from one of these.
Number one, the tutorial angle.
Number two, the due versus down to the right versus wrong,
or the old versus new format.
Number three, the educational tip slash hack format.
Number four, the storytelling format.
Number five, the transformation format.
Number six, the problem solution format.
Number seven, the lists format.
And number eight, the types format.
Step number three, create the carousel.
In Canva, make your reel into a carousel,
and make sure to use the same hook and text
on the cover slide that you did in your reel.
Number four, post.
And when you do add a trending audio,
because this makes it possible to get added to the Rails tab
and get more engagement.
