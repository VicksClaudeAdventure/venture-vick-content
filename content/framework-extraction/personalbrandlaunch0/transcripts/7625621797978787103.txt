What should people do after their first viral video?
The dumb creators just do nothing,
and they keep posting like normal,
but the smart creators break down the viral video
into seven main components.
The verbal or an individual hook,
he structure the format, the editing, the topic,
the value that was used in the video and the CTA.
And with those components,
they templatize the viral video into a repeatable template.
And adjust the value slightly each time
and recreate that over and over and over again
until the video stops performing.
The most successful creators do this.
When my clients get a viral video, we do this.
Because the entire goal when you're growing from scratch
is to get these viral outlier videos.
So you can do this and it makes content creation
and growing a personal brand way easier.
