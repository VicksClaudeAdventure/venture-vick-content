Number one mistake I made with my last app
was not adding Google and Apple login on day one.
I didn't think it would make that big of a difference,
but only 40% of the people who downloaded the app signed up.
Someone who knows way more than me said,
Chris, you need to add Google and Apple sign in, trust me.
And so I did, and immediately the download to sign up rate
went from 40% to 100%.
Literally everyone is choosing this option too.
My apps and users barely choose the email option.
I'm never making this mistake again,
and now every app I build will have this on day one.
Please learn from my mistakes.
Remove every possible barrier between your users and your app.
I build productivity apps and share everything I'm learning here,
so follow along for more tips like this.
