This is my first track race in over 7 months and it didn't go as expect.
You see, I heard my back early in the week and I was actually considering calling off this race
because I didn't want to injure myself badly but I felt better this morning so I chose to run.
I decided to use my favorite race strategy in the 800 which is getting to the front from the start and stay in there
because it makes the competition have to run around if they want to pass and that makes the race a lot harder for them.
I had a decent first lap and honestly felt pretty comfortable at this point and every time someone tried to pass me
I just sped up a bit so I could hold it inside late.
Everything was going to plan inside the last 100 meters where my legs started failing me
and I could hear a second place closing in from behind but I gave it my everything to hold on
and just managed to pull off the wheel.
