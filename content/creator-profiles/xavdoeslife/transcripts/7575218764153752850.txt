You see I saw a big opportunity in the market because ready to drink protein shakes in America are a big deal but there's barely any South African brands doing.

I started testing ingredients because I wanted this drink to taste really good but then I ran into my first problem.
The drink had to be made with milk and milk expires very far which is when I found out that the only way to keep my drink from expiring in a couple of days was to do something called UHD processing which is basically when you heat up the drink to about 140 degrees to kill any of the bacteria.
So now my first plan of making the drink by myself was completely crushed because I definitely could not afford UHD machinery.
I wasn't going to give up that easily though so I started reaching out to milk companies and manufacturers to ask them if they could help me make the drink but the few people that did respond told me that I would need a lot of money to manufacture a drink like this.
So I came up with a new plan. I wanted to sell something smaller that I could actually afford to make until I had enough money to manufacture the drink and that idea flopped as well.
I designed this bottle in board stock hoping I would sell out reasonably fast so that I could buy even more stock to sell but I only ended up selling about half.
