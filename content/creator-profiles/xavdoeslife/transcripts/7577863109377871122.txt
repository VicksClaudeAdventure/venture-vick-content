I tried the first samples for my protein powder brand today and I didn't expect this to
happen.
I had 3 flavours to try which are chocolate, chocolate, mint and vanilla.
I started with the chocolate and I always love chocolate flavoured things so I was expecting
to like it but this is probably the best tasting protein powder I've ever tried.
Moving on to the chocolate mint flavour and I can't lie some mint flavoured things taste
like toothpaste so I wasn't sure this was going to be good but you're all surprised
because I ended up liking this one even more than the plain shop.
Finally it was time to try the vanilla sample and I'm not usually a fan of vanilla protein
powder but I know a lot of people preferred over chocolate so I had to include it and
damn our chocolate because this thing no joke tasted like vanilla ice cream.
I forgot I was drinking protein powder because this genuinely tasted like a vanilla
mochi.
