For Christmas, I build an app so people can make an admin calendar,
send it to their loved ones, and fill it with little surprises.
The app is time zone based, the door will unlock day by day, so they can't cheat.
You get 24 doors to customize one for each day in December.
For example, door one opens on December 1st.
Give cards, pictures, anything really.
So just copy link and share with friends.
