Okay, so you've heard of vision boards, but what about reflection boards?
At the start of every year, I find that we all get really excited to play new goals
that we often forget to look back on how much we actually did the year before.
To start up this year, I wanted to first look back on 2025 and kind of create a digital
keepsake of sorts. For this project, I partnered with Adobe to try out Photoshop for
Touchy Beauty to quickly prepare all my assets for the board. I experimented with different
styles for my photos and I ended up choosing this duo tone and half tone effect.
It was really easy to change the colors and the intensity of the effects using sliders,
directly in Touchy Beauty. And once I was done, I masked the photos to my liking.
To combine my assets and add some text, I opened my images in Photoshop web.
And this is how the final board turned out. Honestly, making this made me realize
I'm really grateful for all the ups and downs that 2025 brought me. It was fun digging
up old travel photos and memories with friends from the year. So, if you're looking for
a fun little activity to start off here, give Photoshop for Touchy Beauty a try and
take your own reflection boards.
