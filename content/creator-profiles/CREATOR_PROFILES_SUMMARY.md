## 3. Cross-Creator Insight — The 4 Highest-Leverage Patterns

**4 of 6 Venture creators** use the same underlying mechanic:

> **Rigidly repeated opening line + rising counter + soft "follow to see" CTA**

Examples:
- @bradjcho: "I can't afford to propose... day [N] of DoorDashing for a diamond ring"
- @keemccomb: "$49K in debt, booked one-way to Alaska, can't leave until I clear $20K... day [N]"
- @yunghunid: "Starting over at 32 with no job, no social life... day [N]"
- @xavdoeslife: Time-anchored "[X days] ago everything went wrong..."

**The counter is the algorithmic engine.** @chida.rajan, @yohnmav, and @noahanderson.14 use the same mechanic in different niches.

### Top 4 Borrowable Patterns (appear in 3+ creators)

| # | Pattern | Creators | Jay's Application |
|---|---------|----------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Day/date-counter serialized hooks** | bradjcho, keemccomb, yunghunid, chida.rajan, yohnmav, noahanderson.14 (6 creators) | Venture series is already day-counted (Day 1 = March 9). Lock a franchise opener. Recovery Series already has counter. |
| 2 | **Signature sign-off mantra** | higherupwellness, daavi, chida.rajan, yohnmav, meshtimes (5 creators) | Jay has NO signature sign-off. Pick one. Repeat on every video. |
| 3 | **Hyper-specific numbers over vague claims** | chida.rajan, noahanderson.14, higherupwellness, chris.raroque, micro.investing (5 creators) | Jay's numbers are already specific ($37.68, $34,776.15). Lean harder — never round, never say "a few." |
| 4 | **"I told the internet I would..." promise-framing** | yohnmav, chida.rajan, higherupwellness (3 creators) | Every Venture/RE update becomes an accountability check-in. Marcus becomes judge, not just viewer. |
