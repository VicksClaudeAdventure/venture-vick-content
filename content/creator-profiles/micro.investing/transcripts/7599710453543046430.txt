My wife and I have a rule.
We are allowed to waste $200 a month,
and neither one of us is able to say a word about it.
We call this our fun money budget.
Trick budgets usually fail because they feel like a prison.
To do this, we have a separate bank account
that receives fun money for each one of us every single month.
By budgeting for guilt-free spending,
we don't micromanage what each other spends,
and we never burn out on saving money.
It saves our budget,
and it can honestly save some marriages
from petty money fights.
Send this to your partner if you need a fun money allowance.
Amy's typically goes to shopping,
and mine normally goes to books.
What would you spend your fun money on?
