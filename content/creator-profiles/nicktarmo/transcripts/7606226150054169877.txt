who used to work at the most sought after companies you can imagine.

These conventionally successful people that you see online are no different than you.
They just found something that they are genuinely interested in and obsess over early on in their life.
And as a result, they become exceptional at whatever they do because it's fun to realize that the only disparity between you and them is not intelligence,
it is simply taking action and starting.
Because they not only found something that they like doing, but they then actually applied themselves in the real world.
So if you ever think that you are not smart enough or good enough to do something incredible, you are wrong.
