# A1 — The Closer You Get to Launching, the More You Want to Quit

**Series:** Authoritative
**Hook category:** Contradiction
**Word count:** 148
**WPM estimate:** 174
**Hook word count:** 13

## Script

Every founder hits a moment where hiding feels safer than shipping. I know because I hit it.

You'd think launching gets easier the more prepared you are. It doesn't. It gets scarier. Because the closer you get to done, the closer you get to judgment.

Week one of building, you're excited. Nobody knows. It's just you and the code.

Week three, the product works. People could use it. And suddenly your brain says — "what if it's not good enough?"

That's not a sign to stop. That's a sign you care about what you're making.

The people who ship aren't the ones without fear. They're the ones who ship WITH fear.

[slow down here] If you're sitting on something finished and you haven't put it out there — this is your sign. Ship it scared.
