## Script

March 9th. Kitchen table. No team. No funding. Just a laptop.

I'd been sitting on this idea for weeks — an app that reads your lease and flags the lines your landlord hopes you'll miss.

I didn't have a co-founder. Didn't have money. Didn't have a CS degree.

What I had was a checklist and an AI tool that said "this is the one worth building."

So I opened my laptop and started.

No dramatic moment. No inspirational music. Just a guy at a kitchen table wondering if he's about to waste a month of his life — or build something real.

[slow down here] That was 29 days ago.

RentShield is live. Real people use it. And it started right here.

Next video: the AI spit back code in 29 seconds — and it actually worked. Follow so you see it.

## Production Layers

- **Spoken Hook:** "March 9th. Kitchen table. No team. No funding. Just a laptop."
- **Visual Hook:** Phone propped on kitchen table filming the laptop screen — raw, unpolished setup. No ring light.
- **Audio Hook:** Ambient room tone. Quiet. Maybe a clock ticking.
- **Text Hook:** "Day 1." in large text, bottom third.
- **Loop:** Opens on the kitchen table, ends with "it started right here" — same table, full circle.

## Delivery Notes
- Speak slowly on the opener. Each phrase is its own beat: "March 9th. [beat] Kitchen table. [beat] No team."
- Em dash pause before "or build something real"
- The closer should feel like you're looking back from the future. Quiet confidence, not hype.
