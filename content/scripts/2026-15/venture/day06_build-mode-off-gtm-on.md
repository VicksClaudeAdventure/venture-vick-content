## Script

What happens when you finish building and have to start selling?

You panic.

March 20th. All 21 build phases — done. The app works. The brand looks professional. Everything I said I'd build, I built.

And I froze.

Because building is comfortable. You're in control. Nobody's judging your code at 2 AM. But selling? That means putting yourself out there. That means rejection. That means finding out if anyone actually WANTS what you made.

I spent three days avoiding it. Tweaking things that didn't need tweaking. "Optimizing" buttons nobody would ever see.

[this is the punchline — land it] I was hiding in my own product.

Most founders don't fail because the product is bad. They fail because selling feels scarier than building — so they never stop building.

Next video: I put it in front of real people. Watching them use it was terrifying. Follow so you don't miss it.
