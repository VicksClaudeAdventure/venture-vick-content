## Script

Fannie Mae says mortgage rates drop to 5.7% by December. Should you wait or buy now?

I bought my property at 6.625%. People told me to wait. I didn't. Here's why.

Waiting for rates to drop sounds smart. But when rates drop, buyers flood back in. More competition. Bidding wars. Sellers raise prices.

You might save $100/month on the mortgage and pay $20,000 more for the house.

"Marry the house, date the rate" — you've heard it. Here's what it actually means. Buy the property when the PRICE is right. Refinance later when rates fall. The rate is temporary. The purchase price is permanent.

I locked in at 6.625%. When rates hit low 5s, I'll refinance. The property I got? Nobody was bidding against me.

[slow down here] The best time to buy is when everyone else is scared to.

Save this for when you're debating whether to make an offer.
