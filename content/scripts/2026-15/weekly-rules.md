# Week 15 — Directional Insights

*Generated from Steps 1-5 research, April 7, 2026*

---

## INSIGHT 1: Lead with vulnerability, not authority
**Source:** @ddennishan "this might sound cheesy" (241K), @chris.raroque "I'm honestly embarrassed" (150K), @meshtimes "still processing" (246K). 4/10 breakouts opened with emotional honesty.
**Where it fits best:** Venture scripts (Jay's build journey has natural vulnerability beats), Authoritative scripts (bold takes hit harder when the speaker acknowledges doubt first).
**Where to skip it:** V-Bucks scripts where the viewer needs to trust the information immediately. Permission slips and number-driven finance content need authority upfront.

## INSIGHT 2: Front-load the insight — reveal in first 15 seconds
**Source:** 7/10 breakouts deliver the core insight within 15 seconds. Zero save the payoff for the end. @chris.raroque "Here's exactly how my app is doing financially" — instant payoff. @raitryna leads with the total, then breaks down categories.
**Where it fits best:** All pillars. Every script this week should reveal the key point in the first 15 seconds.
**Where to skip it:** None — structural rule for the full batch.

## INSIGHT 3: Anger/Injustice is the underused trigger with massive Reddit signal
**Source:** Reddit data shows explosive engagement on injustice topics (W-2→1099: 880 comments, landlord scams, 401K disappearing, credit card theft by family). 0/10 breakout creators used Anger as primary trigger — but the audience wants it.
**Where it fits best:** V-Bucks (W-2→1099, credit score manipulation), Real Estate (what landlords can't legally do, tariff impact), Authoritative (calling out broken systems).
**Where to skip it:** Venture — the build story should stay hopeful.

## INSIGHT 4: "I built this" IS the content — show the product
**Source:** 6/10 breakouts feature someone showing what they built. @ddennishan's AI therapist app demo drove 241K+429K views. @chris.raroque shows dashboards, real revenue. The build IS the content.
**Where it fits best:** Venture scripts. Jay has RentShield — the product should be on screen. Show the app, the dashboard, the actual results.
**Where to skip it:** V-Bucks and Authoritative where teaching is the format.

## INSIGHT 5: The "Identity Tag" hook — ultra-short, tag a tribe
**Source:** "Side projects after work who" (182K), "send this to someone who has high agency" (8.8K/5.2x), "build dating app for crush?" (96K). Hooks under 8 words that tag a specific identity group are breakout catalysts.
**Where it fits best:** 2-3 scripts this week. V-Bucks ("still broke but learning who"), Authoritative ("people who work on themselves in silence who"), Real Estate ("first-time landlords who").
**Where to skip it:** Venture — series narrative needs more context in hooks.
