## Script

You don't need Spotify, Audible, or LinkedIn Learning. Your library card has them free.

I cancelled Spotify a few months ago. Was dealing with ads for weeks until a coworker said — "just use the library app."

I thought she was joking.

She wasn't.

Libby gives you free audiobooks and ebooks. Hoopla gives you free music streaming, movies, and TV shows. LinkedIn Learning is free through most library systems. Kanopy gives you free documentaries and indie films.

Add it up — Spotify is $11. Audible is $15. LinkedIn Learning is $30. That's $56/month you're paying for things your library already covers.

And a library card? Free. You walk in, show your ID, walk out with access to all of it.

[slow down here] $672 a year. Gone. With a free card.

Save this before you pay for next month.

- **Loop:** Opens with subscriptions you're paying for → ends with "$672 gone" — money saved.
