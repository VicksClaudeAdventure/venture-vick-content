# VB1 — Your Boss Switching You to 1099 Is Stealing From You

**Series:** V-Bucks
**Hook category:** Contradiction
**Word count:** 153
**WPM estimate:** 180
**Hook word count:** 13
**Weekly insights applied:** #2 (front-load), #3 (anger/injustice)

---

## Script

Your boss offers you $38/hour instead of $32. Sounds like a raise. It's a pay cut.

Here's what's happening — companies are switching employees from W-2 to 1099. They frame it as more money per hour. But they're cutting your benefits and handing you the tax bill.

As a W-2 at $32/hour, your employer pays half your Social Security and Medicare taxes. That's 7.65% they cover.

As a 1099 at $38/hour, YOU pay both halves. That's 15.3% out of your pocket. Plus no health insurance. No paid time off. No unemployment if they fire you.

Do the math — $38/hour as 1099 after self-employment tax is about $32.19. You gained 19 cents and lost every benefit.

[this is the punchline — land it] That's not a raise. That's your employer saving money and calling it a promotion.

Save this before your boss tries it on you.

## Production Layers

- **Spoken Hook:** "Your boss offers you $38/hour instead of $32. Sounds like a raise. It's a pay cut."
- **Visual Hook:** Calculator app on screen, typing in the numbers in real time.
- **Audio Hook:** Cash register sound on "$38," followed by a "wrong answer" buzzer.
- **Text Hook:** "$38/hr 1099 = $32.19 after tax" — the math exposed.
- **Loop:** Opens with "sounds like a raise" → ends with "calling it a promotion" — same lie, decoded.

## Delivery Notes
- This script is ANGRY. Channel righteous indignation, not whining.
- "7.65%" and "15.3%" — say these clearly. The numbers are the weapon.
- "19 cents" — deadpan. Let the absurdity speak.
- CTA is a save trigger — this is reference content people need when the conversation happens.
