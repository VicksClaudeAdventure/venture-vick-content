## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
Twenty-three DMs sent. Twenty-two ignored. One said "circle back." The counter hasn't moved.

📷 *Phone screen: Instagram DM list — all "sent" with no replies, scrolling fast — 3+ cuts in first 3 seconds*

[Problem — the grind]
After I posted Day 1 I had this energy like okay let's go, let's make this money back. So I did the only thing I knew how to do. I started messaging brands.

📷 *Jay typing on phone, serious face, late night*

[Pursuit — what the grind actually looks like]
Cold DMs on Instagram. "Hey I make content, here's my work, I'd love to do a UGC video for you." Copy. Paste. Send. Over and over. Twenty-three times in one week.

📷 *Screen recording: composing a DM, hitting send, then another, then another — rapid cuts*

[Pursuit — the silence]
Most of them never opened it. A couple left me on read. One said they'd "circle back" which we both know means no.

📷 *Phone screen: "seen" receipts, no response, one reply saying "we'll circle back"*

[Payoff — unresolved but honest]
It's humbling. You put yourself out there over and over and the internet just goes quiet. And you start wondering if you're the problem or if this is just what the beginning looks like.

📷 *Jay looking at phone, then looking at camera — honest, not defeated*

[Counter + CTA]
The counter is still at thirty-four thousand seven hundred seventy-six dollars and fifteen cents. It hasn't moved. But it moves in the next video. Follow to see when it does.
