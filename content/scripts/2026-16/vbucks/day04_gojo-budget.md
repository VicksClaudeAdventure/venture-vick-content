I built his real budget using actual Tokyo cost of living and the 50/30/20 rule. He fails it. Hard.

[Step 1 — needs]
Needs: half his income, sixteen thousand five hundred. Rent in central Shibuya alone eats eight to ten grand. Groceries in Tokyo, transportation, utilities — another six. He's already at the limit before a single fun purchase.

📷 *Dashboard: "Needs: $16,500" with rent, groceries, transit appearing under it*

[Step 2 — wants]
Wants: thirty percent, ten thousand. Gojo is a sweets addict in the most expensive sweet shop district in the world. He's at Higashiya every week. Another ten grand on designer clothes, the blindfold replacements, the driver.

📷 *Dashboard: "Wants: $10,000" with sweets icon, designer clothes, etc.*

[Step 3 — save]
Save: twenty percent. Six thousand five hundred should go into investments. Gojo saves zero dollars.

📷 *Dashboard: "Save: $0 / $6,500" in red*

[Step 4 / CTA]
The strongest sorcerer alive is one bad month from moving back into the dorm. Next episode — Levi Ackerman, lowest-paid captain in the Survey Corps, secretly the richest. Follow.
