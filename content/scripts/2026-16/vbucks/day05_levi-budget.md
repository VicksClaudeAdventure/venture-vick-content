## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
Levi Ackerman is the cleanest freak in Attack on Titan and the only character who'd never run out of money. The math proves it.

📷 *Levi reference image + "cleanest freak" caption + dollar sign on his uniform*

[State the myth]
Everybody assumes the commanders are the rich ones. Erwin with the blonde hair, the big office, the status. And yes — he makes about double what Levi does.

📷 *Erwin image + "$8,400/mo" label*

[Debunk — the math]
But I built both budgets. Erwin spends every dollar on appearances. Levi doesn't.

📷 *Side-by-side budgets: Erwin maxed out / Levi with headroom*

[Debunk — the numbers]
Levi makes forty-two hundred a month as a squad captain. His needs — housing in the barracks, food from the mess, uniform provided — cost him almost nothing. We're talking eight hundred bucks a month, max.

📷 *Dashboard: Needs $800 / Save $3,400*

[Solution — the lesson]
Everything else goes into the Survey Corps equivalent of a savings account. Three thousand four hundred dollars a month, every month, for years.

📷 *Compounding graphic: $3,400/month over 5 years animating up*

[Payoff]
The only thing Levi spends on outside of work is cleaning supplies. And the guy's going to retire with more saved than everyone who outranks him.

📷 *Levi with cleaning rag + bag of money*

[CTA]
Next episode — Denji. Chainsaw Man. Four weeks from eviction. Follow.

📷 *Denji reference image teaser*
