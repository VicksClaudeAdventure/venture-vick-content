## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
Denji from Chainsaw Man is four weeks from eviction while eating toast he thinks is luxury food. I built his real budget.

📷 *Denji reference image + "Week 1 / Week 2 / Week 3 / EVICTION" countdown*

[Mistake]
Denji is sixteen, killing devils for Public Safety, and Japan minimum wage for dangerous work is about fifteen hundred dollars a month. That's his entire income.

📷 *Dashboard: "Income: $1,500/mo" + devil hunter icon*

[Debrief — the math]
His rent in the worst part of Tokyo — because nobody rents to a devil hunter in a nice neighborhood — is eight hundred bucks. Food and transit another five hundred. Gear maintenance two hundred. The math hits zero before anything fun.

📷 *Dashboard: Rent 800, Food/Transit 500, Gear 200 → Left: $0*

[Debrief — the trap]
Now add one thing. One injury. One canceled assignment. Makima holds his paycheck for "reasons." The whole system collapses in weeks.

📷 *Countdown visualization: stack of bills falling over*

[Solution]
This is every early-twenties person with one income and zero buffer. You don't need a complicated plan — you need one month of survival money sitting somewhere you can't easily spend it. Denji doesn't have that. Most of us don't either.

📷 *Text on screen: "1 month of survival = the only rule"*

[CTA]
Next episode — Naruto. He made Hokage. His salary is worse than you think. Follow.

📷 *Naruto reference teaser*
