## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
Naruto Uzumaki became Hokage but he still can't afford ramen every day. Here's the actual math.

📷 *Naruto Hokage robe image + bowl of ramen + price tag*

[Setup]
I researched what a Hokage actually makes. The job is basically mayor plus military commander of a mid-size city. In today's money, that's around ninety thousand dollars a year.

📷 *Text: "Hokage salary ≈ $90,000/year"*

[The math]
Sounds solid. Until you remember he's supporting his whole family on one income. Hinata isn't working. He has two kids, Boruto and Himawari. The Hokage's house is provided, but everything else isn't.

📷 *Family graphic: Naruto → Hinata → Boruto → Himawari, all dependent*

[The walkthrough]
Ninety grand minus taxes — sixty-five take-home. Food for a family of four in a major city — fifteen hundred a month. Kids' school, gear, everything else — another fifteen hundred. Clothing. Utilities. Transportation.

📷 *Dashboard counting down: $65K → ~$26K remaining*

[Lesson]
He's got about twenty-six thousand left for the year. Which sounds fine until you realize that's for everything non-essential. Ramen every day at Ichiraku runs eight bucks. He literally can't do it without cutting somewhere else.

📷 *Ichiraku bowl with $8 price tag*

[Closer]
Becoming the leader of the village didn't fix Naruto's money problem. It just moved it.

📷 *Naruto looking at wallet*

[CTA]
First episode recap and the next four anime next week. Follow if you want me to do your favorite character's budget.

📷 *All 4 characters collaged: Gojo, Levi, Denji, Naruto*
