## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
Founders are burning thousands on things I got for free and I have the receipts to prove it.

📷 *Receipt close-up: total $37.68 — pattern break with number reveal*

[Backstory]
Two weeks ago I built an actual app. Real code, real users, live on the internet. I spent thirty-seven dollars and sixty-eight cents. Here's every category.

📷 *List animating in: 5 categories about to be broken down*

[Value — breakdown]
AI tool to write most of the code — twenty dollars for the one month I needed. Domain name — twelve dollars for the year. Hosting the database — free for the first month, twenty dollars after. Design assets from a free kit — zero. Me working nights in my apartment — also zero.

📷 *Categories listing on screen with their dollar amounts*

[Epiphany]
And before you tell me "you just got lucky" — I know what the same build used to cost. Four grand to a freelancer. Another five hundred for tools. A designer you probably couldn't afford.

📷 *Strike-through: $4,000 + $500 + designer costs*

[Value — the lesson]
The price of getting a real idea off the ground in 2026 is the price of lunch. That's the thing nobody is telling you.

📷 *$37.68 in large text on screen*

[CTA]
Save this. Next video I'm breaking down exactly which tool did what. Follow so you don't miss it.

📷 *Jay at laptop, calm*
