## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
We budgeted twenty thousand for our renovation. We ended up spending over forty-five thousand. I'm gonna show you what happened.

📷 *Budget counter: "$20,000 budget" → then climbing past $25K → $35K → $45K*

[Problem]
We said twenty K. Maybe twenty-five max. New paint, refinish the floors, update some fixtures. Easy. Clean.

📷 *Before photos: rough interior, scuffed walls*

[The bathroom]
Then we opened the bathroom. Quick patch job — that's what we said. Found a leak. Had to tear the whole thing out. New tile. New plumbing. New fixtures. That one room ate through most of the budget by itself.

📷 *Bathroom mid-demo: pipes exposed, tile ripped up*

[Cascade]
Then the dryer broke. Then a gutter ripped off in a storm. Then humidity in the basement nobody told us about. Every single week something else.

📷 *Rapid cuts: broken dryer, damaged gutter, wet basement*

[Result]
Forty-five thousand. One of my friends looked at me and said "bro, you said twenty K."

📷 *After photos: beautiful renovated interior*

[CTA — the payoff]
He was right. But the house is beautiful now. Next video — the full financial breakdown. Every dollar in, what it's worth now. Follow.

📷 *Wide shot: renovated house interior*
