## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
How did three twenty-four-year-olds buy a house, renovate it, and end up forty thousand dollars ahead. I'm gonna show you every receipt.

📷 *House exterior + "$40K ahead" in large text overlay*

[Objection kill]
And no, nobody's parents bought us the down payment.

📷 *Jay direct to camera, flat delivery*

[The investment]
We bought the house for three forty-five. Put down ten percent — about thirty-four five. Spent another forty-five thousand on the renovation. Total cash in: roughly eighty thousand split three ways.

📷 *Calculator animation: $345K + $34.5K down + $45K reno = ~$80K total in*

[The result]
The house appraised at about three eighty-five. That's forty thousand in equity in under two years. Our monthly per person is under a thousand dollars — less than most people pay in rent.

📷 *Comparison bar: $883/mo per person vs. average Philly rent $1,200+*

[The lesson]
Nobody's getting rich off one house. But three people who couldn't afford it alone built something together that's worth more than what they put in. That's the math.

📷 *Final number: $345K → $385K → $40K equity gain*

[CTA]
Next week we're starting the World Cup Airbnb play. The house is thirty minutes from the stadium. Follow if you want to see what we're pricing it at.

📷 *World Cup Philly graphic + 30-minute map*
