## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
I put something I built in front of four strangers. I was not ready for person three.

📷 *Over-shoulder POV: someone holding Jay's app on a phone, scrolling*

[Doubt]
I finally forced myself to send the app to four people who had no idea who I was. Just strangers. Give them a lease, see what they did.

📷 *Jay handing phone across a table, small café setting*

[Struggle — person 1]
Person one pasted her lease. Got six flags in twenty-nine seconds. Nodded. Said "that's actually useful." I almost teared up.

📷 *Hand scrolling through results, close-up of nodding reaction*

[Struggle — person 2]
Person two — same thing. Five flags. Said the tool would've saved him from signing something shady last year.

📷 *Hand on phone, thumbs-up reaction*

[Turning point — person 3]
Person three opened the app and just stared at the upload screen. Then asked me what to do. I told her. She tried. Did the wrong thing. Closed the app.

📷 *Person staring at upload, confused face, closing app*

[Turning point — the gut punch]
The app she closed was the app I was about to ship.

📷 *Jay's face, stomach-drop moment*

[Transformation]
I spent the whole night redoing the upload flow. Again. Because I'd rather keep finding problems now than find them when it's already live.

📷 *Jay at laptop, rebuilding the flow*

[CTA]
Follow to see what I change before I launch this thing for real.
