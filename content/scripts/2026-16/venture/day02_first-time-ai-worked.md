## Voiceover

[Hook]
The first time I ran an AI on my idea, I stared at the screen for a full minute before I believed what was on it.

📷 *Close-up: Jay's face, lit only by screen glow, visible reaction — pattern break*

[Backstory]
So yesterday I told you the AI picked which business I should build. Today I actually started building it.

📷 *Clip: calendar / timestamp "Day 1" on screen*

[Epiphany setup]
I grabbed a real lease — a rental contract from Philly — and I pasted the whole thing into the prompt.

📷 *Screen recording: pasting lease text into the tool*

[Epiphany]
Twenty-nine seconds later it came back with six clauses flagged and a line-by-line breakdown in plain English.

📷 *Screen recording: results loading, red flags popping up with hover text*

[Value]
I didn't write any of that. I didn't even know which clauses were supposed to be sketchy. But it knew.

📷 *Jay shaking head at screen, slight disbelief*

[Soft CTA]
There was still so much wrong with it. I'm gonna spend the next week finding every hole in this thing. Tomorrow I'm building the overnight sprint that turned the whole project. Follow if you want to see how it breaks.

📷 *Jay at laptop, late-night lighting*
