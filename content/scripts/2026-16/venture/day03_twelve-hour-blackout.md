## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
Day six of building alone and I don't remember half of what I built last night but it works.

📷 *Time-lapse: dark room with laptop glow → slow pan → dawn light through window*

[Agitate]
I can't afford a cofounder. I can't afford a designer. I can't afford to outsource any of this.

📷 *Jay at laptop, alone, notebook beside keyboard*

[Re-hook]
So yesterday at 9 PM I sat down to fix one bug.

📷 *Clock on phone: 9:14 PM*

[Context]
I hadn't eaten since lunch. I hadn't checked my phone in hours. I just kept typing.

📷 *Time-lapse: laptop screen, hands typing, empty water bottle appearing*

[Solution — the blackout]
When I looked up, it was seven in the morning. Twenty-one new features in the app. Onboarding, pricing, settings, the whole back end — built.

📷 *Screen recording: app with new features, text overlay "21 phases in 10 hours"*

[Peak end]
I'm not gonna pretend I remember writing any of it. And I'm genuinely scared to open some of those files to see what's inside. But the app works.

📷 *Jay leaning back in chair, morning light on his face*

[CTA]
Tomorrow I'm showing you what I found when I actually read the code. Follow if you want to see me react to my own work in real time.

📷 *Jay at laptop, slightly tired smile*
