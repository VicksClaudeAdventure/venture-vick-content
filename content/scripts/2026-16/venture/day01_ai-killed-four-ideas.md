## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
Five ideas. No money. No cofounder. I asked an AI to kill four of them in twelve minutes.

📷 *Visual hook: 5 notes app items rapidly appearing, then 4 crossing out in red — 3+ cuts in first 3 seconds*

[Problem — backstory]
I'd been staring at these five for weeks like a toxic ex I couldn't commit to.

📷 *Jay at kitchen table, late-night lighting, 5 post-its visible — library clip*

[Pursuit — the action]
So I dumped all five into an AI and said — don't hype me up, tell me which one would actually work.

📷 *Screen recording: typing the five ideas into an AI chat*

[Pursuit — the method]
It scored them on three things. Would people pay for it. How crowded the space was. Could I build it alone.

📷 *Close-up: AI response with 3-column ranking, numbers ticking*

[Payoff — the result]
Four got killed in twelve minutes. The only one left was the idea I almost didn't put on the list.

📷 *Screen: 4 ideas crossed out in red, 1 highlighted green*

[CTA — teaser + soft follow]
Tomorrow I'm showing you the first time I ran it on a real lease. I stared at the screen for a full minute before I believed what came back. Follow to see what it gave me.

📷 *Jay looking at camera, quiet nod — reflective mood*
