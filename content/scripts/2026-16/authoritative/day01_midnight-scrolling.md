## Voiceover

[Hook — 0:00-0:04]
If you're watching this at midnight hating yourself for scrolling again, I know exactly what you're going through.

📷 *Jay in dim lighting, face lit by phone glow, direct to camera — instant mirror*

[Backstory]
I'm twenty-four. I have things I want to build, people I want to become, goals I think about every single day. And then I spend the night on this app doing nothing about any of it.

📷 *Phone showing screen time stats: 4+ hours social media*

[Agitate — the shame spiral]
And the worst part isn't the wasting time. It's that I KNOW I'm doing it. I can see it happening. I can feel the night disappearing. And I still don't put the phone down.

📷 *Jay looking down at phone, then looking back at camera*

[Epiphany]
So six months ago I stopped trying to fix the scrolling and started replacing it with something. I started building an app. I started making content instead of consuming it. I bought a house with my friends. And none of that made the scrolling stop. But now at least some of my hours go somewhere that matters.

📷 *Quick flash: app screenshot, filming content, the house*

[Value — the honest truth]
I'm not fixed. I still brain rot. I still procrastinate. But I'm also building things now, and that didn't happen until I stopped waiting to feel ready and just started.

📷 *Jay direct to camera, quiet*

[CTA]
Follow to watch me figure this out in real time. Because I'm definitely still figuring it out.

📷 *Jay slight shrug, genuine*
