## Voiceover

I finally sat down and used my own app like a real person would and I'm not gonna lie I hated five things about it.

📷 *Split screen: Jay as "user" on one side, the app on the other*

Like the AI built this thing and it worked, technically. But when I actually tried to upload a lease and go through the results? It felt off.

📷 *Screen recording showing the clunky original UI*

The upload flow was confusing. The results page was cluttered. The language was too technical — like who's gonna know what "material breach" means? The colors had no contrast. And the loading screen looked like a placeholder from 2015.

📷 *Quick cuts between each of the 5 issues*

So I fixed all five of them that same day. And that's the moment it stopped being "the AI built this" and started being "I built this."

📷 *Side by side: before and after UI*

[laid back here] Because the AI gives you the foundation but the five things you change are what make people actually trust it.

I'm documenting everything about building this app. Follow if you're curious what happens next.
