## Voiceover

I finished building the app and I'm not gonna lie I spent three days straight avoiding the next step.

📷 *Jay at desk, staring at screen, not typing — procrastination energy*

All twenty-one build phases done. The app works. The brand looks professional. Everything I said I was gonna build, I built. And then I just... froze.

📷 *Screen showing completed app dashboard*

Because building is comfortable right? Nobody's judging your code at 2 AM. But selling means putting yourself out there. It means people can say no. Or worse — they just don't care.

📷 *Jay scrolling through his phone, not posting*

[laid back here] So I spent three days tweaking buttons nobody would ever see and "optimizing" things that were already fine. And I realized I was just hiding in my own product.

📷 *Close-up of Jay finally clicking "publish" or "share"*

Most people don't fail because what they built is bad. They fail because selling feels scarier than building so they just never stop building.

I'm documenting everything about building this app. Follow if you're curious what happens next.
