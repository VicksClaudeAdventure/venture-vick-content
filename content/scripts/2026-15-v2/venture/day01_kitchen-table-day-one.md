## Voiceover

So on March 9th I'm sitting at my kitchen table with literally nothing — no team, no money, no plan.

📷 *Jay at actual kitchen table, laptop open, raw setup — no ring light, no studio*

I'd been thinking about this app idea for weeks where you just paste your lease and it tells you what's sketchy in plain English. And I kept talking myself out of it.

📷 *Phone screen showing notes app with the idea written out*

Like who am I to build this? I don't have a CS degree. I don't have funding. I'm just some dude at a kitchen table.

📷 *Close-up of Jay's hands on keyboard*

[laid back here] But I finally just said forget it, I'm building this today. And I opened my laptop and started typing.

📷 *Screen recording of the first project file being created*

[pause] That was 29 days ago. RentShield is live now. Real people use it. And it all started right here at this table.
