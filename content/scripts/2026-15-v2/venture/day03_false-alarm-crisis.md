## Voiceover

So I'm not gonna lie, my AI was flagging things wrong eighty-four percent of the time and I was freaking out.

📷 *Screen recording showing the app with red flags EVERYWHERE — visual overload*

I built this app that's supposed to read your lease and flag the risky stuff right? Well it was flagging EVERYTHING. Normal lines, standard terms, stuff that was completely fine.

📷 *Close-up of results page showing false positives*

Eighty-four percent false positive rate. That means if it flagged ten things, eight of them were wrong. The product was basically useless.

📷 *Jay's face — genuine frustration*

And I'm sitting there like — did I just waste two weeks of my life?

[beat] So I rebuilt the entire scoring system from scratch. Rewrote every rule. Tested it against ninety-nine different leases.

📷 *Screen showing test results, numbers improving*

[pause] Got it down to eight percent. Same AI, same app — just better instructions.

Tomorrow I'm showing you the five things I changed that made it feel like MY app instead of just something an AI built. Follow if you're curious.
