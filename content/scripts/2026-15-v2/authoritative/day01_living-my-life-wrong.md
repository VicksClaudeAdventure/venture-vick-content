## Voiceover

I was today years old when I realized I'm living my life wrong.

📷 *Jay sitting somewhere real — his room, his car, his kitchen. Not a studio.*

I'm twenty-four and I feel imprisoned by the desire to brain rot every single day. Like I have so much untapped potential to become one of the greats within my family and I'm wasting it scrolling TikTok at midnight.

📷 *Phone screen showing screen time stats or social media feed*

And the worst part is I KNOW I'm doing it. It's not like I'm unaware. I see myself choosing the easy thing over the hard thing every single day and I'm tired of it.

📷 *Jay looking at camera, raw*

[beat] So I started building instead. I built an app. I started creating content. I bought a house with my friends. And I'm not saying I'm fixed — I still brain rot, I still procrastinate — but at least now I'm doing something with the time I'm not wasting.

📷 *Quick montage: app, content setup, house*

[laid back here] If you feel like you're wasting your potential, you probably are. But you can start right now.

Follow me because I'm figuring this out in real time.
