## Voiceover

I spent four years consuming content about building a business and then I actually built one in two weeks.

📷 *Jay's phone showing podcast apps, YouTube history, book shelf — the consumption phase*

Like I read every startup book. Listened to every podcast on my commute. Followed every founder on Twitter. I genuinely thought I was preparing myself.

📷 *Stack of books, headphones, Twitter feed*

[beat] I was not even close. Because the things I learned in two weeks of actually building were completely different from everything I'd read about.

📷 *Jay at his laptop, building*

Your first users don't care about your features — they care if it works. Your pricing is wrong the first time, guaranteed. And the hardest part isn't the code, it's telling people that your thing exists.

📷 *Quick cuts: user feedback, pricing page, social media post*

[laid back here] Four years of consuming gave me confidence. Two weeks of building gave me competence. And those are not the same thing.

Follow me because I'm still learning this stuff every day.
