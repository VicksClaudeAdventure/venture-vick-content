## Voiceover

I'm trying to refinance my house from 6.625% and get someone removed from the loan at the same time and it's way more complicated than I expected.

📷 *Jay looking at refinance paperwork or lender website*

So we bought the property with three people on the mortgage right? Well now one person needs to come off the loan. And when you refinance, you can do both at once — new rate and new borrower lineup.

📷 *Simple diagram: 3 people → 2 people + lower rate*

But here's what I'm learning. First you need an appraisal to see what the house is actually worth now. Then the remaining borrowers have to re-qualify on their own. And the fees add up — closing costs, appraisal, title insurance.

📷 *List of costs appearing*

[beat] We're targeting the low fives. If rates hit 5.3 or 5.4, our monthly payment drops by about [$ amount]. That's real money.

📷 *Before/after payment comparison*

This is happening in real time. I'll show you every step as it plays out. Follow so you don't miss it.
