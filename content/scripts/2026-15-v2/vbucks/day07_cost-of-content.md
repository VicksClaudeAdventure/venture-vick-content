## Voiceover

Everyone shows you the brand deals but nobody shows you what it actually costs to be a content creator.

📷 *Jay surrounded by his content creation setup — camera, lights, mic, laptop*

So I'm gonna break it down because I think people need to hear this. My phone — which is my main camera — was [$ amount]. Ring light was [$ amount]. Microphone was [$ amount]. Editing software is [$ amount] a month.

📷 *Each item showing up with its price tag*

Then there's the stuff you don't see. Hours editing. Hours scripting. Hours researching. If I charged myself minimum wage for the time I spend on content, it would be [$ amount] a month.

📷 *Clock graphic showing hours invested*

[beat] And the biggest cost nobody talks about? The opportunity cost. Every hour I'm filming is an hour I'm not doing freelance work or building my app.

📷 *Jay looking at camera, honest*

I'm not complaining. I chose this. But if you think content creation is free because you already have a phone — it's not.

Save this if you're thinking about starting.
