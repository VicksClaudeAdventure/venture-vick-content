## Voiceover

I let an AI decide which business to build and it picked the one idea I almost left off the list.

📷 *Screen showing a chat with 5 business ideas listed*

So I had five ideas and I couldn't pick one. I'd been going back and forth for weeks so I just dumped all five into an AI and said don't hype me up, just tell me what would actually work.

📷 *AI response scoring the ideas — zoom on the rankings*

It scored them on would people pay, how crowded is it, can I build it alone. Four of them got eliminated in twelve minutes. And the winner was RentShield — you paste your lease and it tells you what's sketchy in plain English.

📷 *The RentShield concept on screen*

[beat] And what got me is that RentShield wasn't even my favorite idea. I almost didn't include it on the list. But the AI was right.

📷 *Screen recording: uploading a lease, results coming back in 29 seconds*

The next day I built the first version and it analyzed a full lease in twenty-nine seconds. It was rough but it existed and that changed everything for me.

I'm documenting everything about building this app, follow if you're curious what happens next.
