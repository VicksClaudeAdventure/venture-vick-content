## Voiceover

I'm honestly embarrassed to admit this but I finished building my app and then I hid from the world for three days.

📷 *Jay at desk, staring at screen, hands off keyboard*

Like all twenty-one build phases were done. The app worked. The brand looked professional. Everything I said I was gonna build, I built. And then I just froze.

📷 *Screen showing completed app — ready to launch*

Because building is safe right? Nobody's judging your code at 2 AM. But selling means putting yourself out there. It means someone can look at this thing you spent weeks on and go "this is trash."

📷 *Jay scrolling his phone, not posting*

[beat] So I spent three days tweaking buttons nobody would ever click and optimizing pages nobody would ever see. And at some point I realized I wasn't perfecting the product — I was just scared.

📷 *Jay finally opening a new tab, starting to draft a post*

Most people who build stuff don't fail because the product is bad. They fail because shipping feels scarier than building so they just keep building forever.

I'm documenting everything about building this app, follow if you're curious what happens next.
