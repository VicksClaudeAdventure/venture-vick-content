A brand DM'd me offering two hundred and fifty dollars for a video and I never even reached out to them.

📷 *Phone showing Instagram DM notification — the actual message*

So remember Day 2? I sent twenty-something cold DMs to brands and basically got ignored by everyone. I was starting to think this whole thing wasn't gonna work.

📷 *Quick flash of Day 2 — the unanswered DMs*

Then I open Instagram one morning and there's a message from a brand called Zapply. They saw my content and wanted a JJK-style video — anime editing, clean cuts, motion effects. That's literally my thing. I already make those.

📷 *Jay filming or editing the JJK-style video*

[beat] They didn't find me because I pitched them. They found me because I was posting consistently and my work showed up on their feed. That's the part that hit me — the outreach I did in Day 2 didn't land the first deal. The content did.

📷 *Payment notification: $250*

Two hundred and fifty dollars. Is it life changing? No. But someone paid me for what I already do without me asking them to. And that's proof the strategy works.

📷 *Recovery counter dropping: $34,776.15 → $34,526.15*

The counter moved. $34,526 and 15 cents to go. Follow so you see the next one.

📷 *End card: $34,526.15 TO GO*
