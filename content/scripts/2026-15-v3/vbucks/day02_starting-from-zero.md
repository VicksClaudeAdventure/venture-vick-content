## Voiceover

If I lost everything tomorrow and had to start over, these are the first three things I'd do with my money.

📷 *Jay sitting somewhere casual — his room, a coffee shop*

And I'm not speaking hypothetically because I kind of already did this. I built a whole app for thirty-seven bucks and bought a house with two friends because none of us could afford one alone.

📷 *Quick flash: RentShield app, the house*

So number one — I'd stop paying for anything I can get free. I cancelled subscriptions I wasn't using, I found free tools to build with, and I stopped spending money to look like I had money.

📷 *Phone showing cancelled subscriptions*

Number two — I'd find two or three people to split a big investment with. For me that was the house. Solo I couldn't do it. Together we could.

📷 *The three friends or the house*

Number three — I'd start building something that costs almost nothing to launch. An app, content, a service. Something where the main investment is time, not cash.

📷 *Jay at laptop*

[beat] You don't need a pile of money to start. You need a plan that doesn't require one.

Save this for when you're ready to start.
