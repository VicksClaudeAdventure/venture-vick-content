## Voiceover

I bought a camera for my content and someone told me I'm wasting my money — and that was the nicest comment.

📷 *Jay holding up a piece of content equipment*

Like I invested in gear for my rental property and someone said "why are you spending money on that." I paid twelve dollars for a domain name for my app and someone had an opinion about THAT. Twelve dollars.

📷 *Quick montage of the items people judged*

And I've noticed something about the people who comment on how you spend your money — they're telling you way more about their relationship with money than yours.

📷 *Jay looking at camera directly*

[beat] Someone who's comfortable financially doesn't track what you buy. Someone who's stressed about money notices everything you spend.

📷 *Jay casually using his equipment, working on content*

I used to explain every purchase. Now I don't. And honestly that was one of the best financial decisions I've made — just spending on what matters to me and stopping the explanations.

Comment "my money" if you deal with this.
