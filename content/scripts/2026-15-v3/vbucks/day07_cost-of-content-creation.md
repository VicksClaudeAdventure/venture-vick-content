## Voiceover

The number one mistake new creators make is not knowing what it actually costs to create content before they start.

📷 *Jay surrounded by his setup — camera, light, mic, laptop*

Everyone sees the brand deals and thinks this is free money. But here's what nobody shows you. My phone which is my main camera was [$ amount]. Ring light [$ amount]. Microphone [$ amount]. Editing software is [$ amount] a month.

📷 *Each item with price tag appearing*

Then there's the invisible cost — time. If I charged myself minimum wage for the hours I spend filming, editing, and scripting, it'd be [$ amount] a month. And that's time I'm not freelancing or building my app.

📷 *Clock showing hours — filming, editing, planning*

[beat] And the biggest one nobody talks about is opportunity cost. Every hour I'm creating content is an hour I'm not doing something else that pays right now.

📷 *Jay looking at camera honestly*

I'm not complaining — I chose this and I'd choose it again. But if you think content is free because you have a phone, run the real numbers first.

Save this if you're thinking about starting.
