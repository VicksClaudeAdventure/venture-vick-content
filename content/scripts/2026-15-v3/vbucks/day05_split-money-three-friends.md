📷 *Simple diagram: 3 people → 1 property → money flowing*

So me and two friends own a rental property together and the number one question I get is how do you actually handle the money. Because it sounds messy. And honestly it kind of is, but we've figured out a system.

📷 *The system — spreadsheet or shared account view*

The mortgage payment comes out of one shared account. We each transfer our third on the first of every month — [$ amount] each. Insurance and taxes same thing — split three ways, auto-transfer.

📷 *Transfer notifications on phone*

When something breaks we discuss it in our group chat, agree on the fix, and split the cost. Last month it was [specific repair] for [$ amount] — that's [$ amount] each.

📷 *Group chat screenshot showing the discussion*

[beat] When rent comes in it sits in the shared account and covers the next month's expenses. Whatever's left over gets split equally.

📷 *The math on screen — clean and simple*

The key honestly is having everything automated and documented so nobody has to ask for money.
