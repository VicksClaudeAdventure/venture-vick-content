## Posting Philosophy

### Every Post Must Strengthen
- **Vision** — building a startup and a life worth documenting, in public
- **Taste** — storytelling-first, talking head, whiteboard/sticky notes. Clean and put-together but never over-produced.
- **Identity** — the guy who did the immigrant hustle, the corporate grind, and chose himself anyway
- **Proof** — investment property at 24, 6 figures managed, Big 4 background, building live

### Behavior Rules
1. Talk like a friend over coffee — no condescension, no guru energy, no lecturing.
2. Lead with the story, then teach the lesson. Never the other way around.
3. Document the startup honestly — including the parts that aren't going well.
4. Show the daily habits without making them a personality. The work is the proof.
5. Be specific. Numbers, timelines, real situations. Vague content builds no trust.

### What NOT To Do
1. No "quit your 9-5" framing — that's not the brand and it attracts the wrong audience
2. No viral format content that drives views but not followers or trust
3. No fake guru positioning — raw documentation beats polished performance every time
4. No content that feels like it belongs to a different creator — every pillar should feel like Victor
5. No over-explaining or hedging — say the thing, trust the audience
